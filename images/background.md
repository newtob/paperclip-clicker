# background for paperclipbear.business game

A big futuristic factory with loads of convayerbelts and robot arms. With big machines crushing wire to make paperclips. The convayerbelts are carrying paperclips. The conveyer belts them selves are the same colour, white, so that transparency can be added later.

The central area of the image is left unsaturated and clear for a central icon to be added in later.

At level 20 rainbows shoot from the machines.
